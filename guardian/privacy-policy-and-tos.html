Privacy Policy

    * We use your name, email address and captured photos so that we can send the information to you through the alert mail.

    * We do not use any of your data for any purpose other than serving you better. We may have to share some information with some trusted third parties for the sole purpose of serving you using thier service (like email sending service). But rest assured, none of this information is up for sale.

    We have only the best intentions for the privacy of our users so we hope this satisfies your curiosity.
